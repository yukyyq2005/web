About Wizard Tackle

Wizard Tackle is Australian owned and operated, focussing on providing the best possible equipment for Australian Fisherman. From novices to those who have spent more time on boats than land – Wizard Tackle focusses on providing a large range of tackle and gear to fit their needs.

Steve Evans – Wizard Tackles’ general manager has been a driving force in moving from the standard run of the mill tackle to providing innovative products that put Wizard Tackle in the forefront of the Fishing Tackle industry.
